# Projection and Polarity (Mock)

Projection can happen when a person attributes an unwanted trait to others and reacts with unusual intensity.
The outer judgment may carry inner significance.

Common polarity patterns:

- closeness vs autonomy
- freedom vs safety
- tenderness vs control
- humility vs superiority

Journaling clues:

- repeated irritation toward similar personalities
- absolutist language ("always", "never", "everyone")
- contradictory wishes in the same entry

Integration direction:

1. Name the criticized trait in the other person.
2. Ask where this trait exists in potential form in oneself.
3. Hold both poles without forcing immediate resolution.
