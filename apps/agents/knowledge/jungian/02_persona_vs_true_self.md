# Persona vs Authentic Self (Mock)

Persona is the social mask used to function in groups, institutions, and roles.
It is useful and necessary, but can become rigid.

A mismatch may appear when a person says:

- "I should be grateful, but I feel empty."
- "Everyone thinks I am strong, but I feel fragile."
- "I am successful, yet disconnected from my own values."

Shadow pressure can increase when persona identity is overinvested.
The person may defend image at the cost of emotional truth.

Reflective direction:

- What role am I performing?
- What feeling is excluded by this role?
- What fear appears if I drop the mask, even briefly?
